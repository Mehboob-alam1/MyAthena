- Emotions are predictions, not reactions
- The brain constructs emotions from:
  - Interoception (body sensations)
  - Past experiences (memory)
  - Current context (situation)
  - Concepts/labels (language)
- We can reconstruct our emotional responses by changing concepts

**Application Strategy:**
- **Oracle:** Help users reframe emotional questions
