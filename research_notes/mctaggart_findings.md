### 4. How to Set Powerful Intentions
**The 8-Step Intention Process:**

1. **Get into a meditative state** - quiet the mind, slow breathing
2. **Focus on compassion** - generate feelings of love and care
3. **Visualize the target** - see the person/situation clearly
4. **State the intention** - specific, positive, present tense
5. **Visualize the outcome** - see it as already accomplished
6. **Feel the feeling** - emotionally experience the success
7. **Release attachment** - let go of the outcome
8. **Trust the field** - know the intention is working

**Keys to Effective Intention:**
- **Specificity** - clear, detailed, unambiguous
- **Present tense** - "is" not "will be"
- **Positive framing** - what you want, not what you don't want
- **Emotional charge** - feeling amplifies intention
- **Belief** - doubt blocks intention
- **Detachment** - desperation weakens intention
